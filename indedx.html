Manifesto

The purpose isn't necessarily to be a business venture (although, that's a welcome side effect), the intention is to be/provide an outlet for conceiving creative ideas, experiment with reckless abandon, and implement ideas in the most intriguing and mind-bending manner possible.

In essence, Early Warning Studio is a collective created to satisfy our curiosities while making the lives of others a little better than it's been.

Structure
  • 5 Founding members with equal ownership and rights.
  • Each founding member can independently experiment and execute on an idea that's interesting to them.
  • Founding members can collaborate to bring an idea to life.
  • Any founding member can bring in external collaborators to work on an idea they care about(they're going to be responsible for any cost incurred by the external collaborators)
  • No OKRs, no KPIs, just vibes and doing meaningful and interesting stuff.
  • Complete autonomy of the founding members
Processes
  • Ideation
  • Research if required
  • Share with founding team if you want/need to
  • Designs if required
  • Build
  • Ship
  • Write about it
  • Relax
NB: There are no external stakeholders, you are the stakeholder.
Funding & Compensation
  • The collective funds itself.
  • Basically, create a budget, and everyone contributes funds your crazy idea
  • Monthly fee?
  • Donations.
  • Equal stakes.
